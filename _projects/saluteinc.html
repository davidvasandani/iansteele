The Challenge

SALUTE helps deliver short term financial assistance to bridge the temporary income gaps and meet the basic needs of post 9/11 veterans. They came to us for a redesign of their outdated existing website. With non-tech savvy users maintaining their website, they needed a wordpress site that made it easy to share stories and upcoming events.
